About Me

Hi my name is Samy El-Jisri. I have lived and worked in the Greater Toronto Area for my entire career. I've been working at TD Bank for the past 3+ years and I'm currently working as a Senior BI and Reporting Analyst. My role has me splitting my duties between daily reporting tasks and working on projects to improve our reporting capabilities within the department. I am looking forward to continuing my development both from expanding my knowledge of the bank and also my technical capaiblities.
